Understanding Difficulty Levels

🟢 Beginner

Calm waters, scenic floats, minimal paddling experience needed. Perfect for families, children, and first‑time rafters. Our guides handle the steering while you enjoy the view.

🟠 Intermediate

Moderate rapids with some maneuvering. Good for those with basic experience or a spirit for moderate adventure. You'll paddle actively and feel the rush without extreme danger.

🔴 Pro

Intense rapids, precise paddling, and continuous action. Requires previous rafting experience, good physical condition, and the ability to follow rapid commands instantly.
