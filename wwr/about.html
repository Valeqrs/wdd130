Venice Rafting

Our mission in Venice Rafting is to deliver unforgettable whitewater adventures inspired by Venezuela, "Little Venice." Though Caracas has no rapids, we run expert trips to the country's best rivers, plus paddling sports on the capital's calm waters. Oars up!

History

Venice Rafting launched with just two rafts and a fierce commitment to adventure. Over the years, we’ve grown into a premier rafting outfitter, guiding thousands of explorers through legendary canyons. Our heritage is rooted in safety, environmental stewardship, and the pure joy of untamed water. From humble riverside beginnings to multi-state expeditions, we continue to write our story on every rapid.

Our name, "Venice Rafting," is inspired by Venezuela, a country once called "Little Venice" by early explorers. While the capital city of Caracas does not have whitewater rivers suitable for rafting, the spirit of adventure runs deep. Excellent organized trips depart from Caracas to the best rafting rivers in the country. Alternatively, visitors and locals can enjoy paddling sports such as kayaking and rowing on the calm waters within the capital. Just as Venezuela blends urban energy with wild landscapes, Venice Rafting brings the rush of the river to anyone seeking the thrill of the current.
